 Project proposal (10%) (2-3 pages):
    • Problem Statement: What are you solving? Why is it interesting/important?
    • Dataset: What data will you use? Source, size, preprocessing needed. Show sample data.
    • Methodology: What models/techniques? Why these choices? Architecture diagram.
    • Evaluation Plan: What metrics? Baseline comparisons. Success criteria.
    • Ethics & Limitations: Potential biases. Limitations of approach. Responsible use considerations
